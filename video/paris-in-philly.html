Paris in Philly

A case of wanderlust inspired this "Paris in Philly" vlog. Without a plane ticket at my disposal, I challenged myself to create a Parisian vlog from my home in Philly. After an evening of location planning, a day of shooting, and a day of editing, the vlog was completed.

Locations shot, in order of appearance:

  • Petit Jardin En Ville, a Parisian floral shop
  • Parc Bistro & Cafe, a Parisian brasserie
  • Rittenhouse Square, designed by French architect Paul Philippe Cret
  • Rodin Museum, the largest collection of sculptor Auguste Rodin’s works outside Paris
  • Benjamin Franklin Parkway, often called Philadelphia’s Champs-Élyées, designed by French architect Jacques Greber
  • Miel Patisserie, a Parisian patisserie
