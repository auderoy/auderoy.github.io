Connecting students to Philly

Penn is located in University City, just a few minutes outside Center City Philadelphia. Despite the close proximity, students find themselves stuck in the college bubble and don't take as much advantage of the city as they could, missing out on potentially enriching experiences.

How might we encourage Penn students to know the City of Brotherly Love and Penn's place in it?

Art of the Web at University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA (May 2018)
Supervised by Sebastien Derenoncourt, Professor
