Tri-Co Womxn of Color Conference

This poster was designed for the Tri-Co Womxn of Color Conference at Haverford College based off a logo commissioned from a student artist.

Bold elements, a purple color scheme, and a clear call to action emphasise the strength and intersectionality of womxn of color and encourage the womxn of color of our campuses to celebrate and discuss their unique experiences at this event.

This poster was attached to all digital marketing materials including for emails, newsletters, e-bulletins, and e-calendars, and was printed for distribution across the Tri-Co (Bryn Mawr, Haverford, and Swarthmore Colleges). I also designed and developed a simple one page scroll website.
