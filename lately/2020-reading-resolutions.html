iii. Give backlist titles more love. Don't be such a clout chaser. Stop seeking validation from publishers.

iv. Don't be afraid to DNF. You know a book has got to go when you start feeling resentful toward it. Remind yourself of how good books can be by reflecting on your top reads of 2019.

v. Read what's already on your shelf. Which leads to the next resolution:

vi. Buy as few books as you can bear. Remember, you used to read a lot of ebooks and borrow a lot from the library. Exceptions: books that made a deep impact on you, unique editions.

vii. Read less thrillers.
